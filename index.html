Long story short:

I write blogs, scripts, emails, landing pages, love letters, and anything that needs a good sentence.

Long story longer:

I would love to romanticize my story and tell you how words found me at the age of seven and how I was dissecting Shakespeare poems while the neighbourhood kids were out playing football.

But none of that is true.

Growing up, I never liked writing or reading. In fact, the only time I put pen to paper was during exams. But life, as it turns out, had a funny way of proving me wrong. One thing led to another, and by the end of 2024 I found myself seriously thinking about writing as a career.

In January 2025, I joined a digital marketing agency, and since then I have worked with 15+ clients, learned to meet deadlines I did not think were possible, and realised that clear client communication matters just as much as the writing itself.

Anyway, below I have gathered some of my best work.

Make yourself at home and look around, it'll tell you more than I can.
